Welcome to the City of Tooele, Utah

Utah's brightest Star!

Tooele City was incorporated on January 21, 1853 and named county seat on January 18, 1861. Tooele was primarily an agricultural community and grew to a population of about 1,200 at the turn of the century. Many of the prominent families who settled Tooele have descendants living in the area.

  • Tooele was a testing ground for the United States growing aresonal of chemical and biological weapons in the 1940s.
  • The Bonneville Salt Flats are just outside the city providing over 30,000 acres of flat high speed fun.
  • Tooele is a growing city with lots of local resturants to try!
